Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I track my order?

You can track your order in real-time through the RushMedz app. Go to "My Orders" and tap on the active order to see the live location of your driver and estimated delivery time.

What are your delivery hours?

Our delivery service operates 24/7 in Tuguegarao City and major cities. In other areas, delivery hours may vary from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Check the app for availability in your area.

How do I upload a prescription?

In the app, tap "Upload Prescription" from the home screen or when adding prescription medicines to your cart. Take a clear photo of your prescription and submit. Our pharmacists will verify it before processing your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept GCash, PayMaya, credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and Cash on Delivery (COD). All payment methods are secure and encrypted.

How do I become a partner pharmacy?

Download the RushMedz Merchant app and complete the registration form. You'll need to submit your FDA license, business permits, and other required documents. Our team will verify and onboard you within 1-3 business days.
