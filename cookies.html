Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how RushMedz uses cookies and similar tracking technologies on our website and mobile applications. By using our services, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit websites or use apps. They help websites remember information about your visit, making your next visit easier and the site more useful.

Types of Cookies

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device until deleted or expired
  • First-Party Cookies: Set by RushMedz directly
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services we use (analytics, advertising)

2. How We Use Cookies

2.1 Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the website and apps to function properly. You cannot disable them.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
rushmedz_session Maintains your login session Session
auth_token Authenticates your account 30 days
csrf_token Protects against security attacks Session
device_id Identifies your device for security 1 year

2.2 Performance & Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our platform, allowing us to improve performance and user experience.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration

These cookies enable enhanced functionality and personalization.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness.

3. Similar Technologies

3.1 Local Storage

We use browser local storage to save data locally on your device for faster app performance and offline functionality.

3.2 Web Beacons/Pixels

Small transparent images embedded in emails and web pages to track opens, clicks, and user behavior.

3.3 Mobile SDKs

Software development kits in our mobile apps that collect usage data and enable features like push notifications and analytics.

3.4 Device Fingerprinting

We collect device information (OS, screen size, browser type) to detect fraud and ensure security.

5. Cookie Management

5.1 Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

5.2 Mobile App Settings

In the RushMedz app:

  1. Go to Settings → Privacy → Cookie Preferences
  2. Toggle cookie categories on/off (except essential cookies)
  3. Save your preferences

5.3 Opt-Out Tools

  • Google Analytics Opt-out: Browser Add-on
  • Facebook Opt-out: Ad Preferences in Facebook settings
  • Do Not Track: Enable in browser settings (honored on our website)

5.4 Cookie Preference Center

Click the cookie icon in the bottom right corner of our website to manage your preferences at any time.

6. Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain cookies may affect your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Website/app may not function properly
  • Performance Cookies: We can't improve based on usage data
  • Functionality Cookies: Personalization features won't work
  • Marketing Cookies: You may see less relevant advertisements

7. Cookie Consent

7.1 First Visit

When you first visit our website or app, we'll display a cookie banner asking for your consent. You can:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences

7.2 Withdrawing Consent

You can withdraw or change your cookie consent at any time through:

  • Cookie Preference Center on website
  • App Settings → Privacy → Cookies
  • Browser cookie settings

8. International Users

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), UK, or other regions with specific cookie laws:

  • We obtain explicit consent before setting non-essential cookies
  • You have the right to withdraw consent at any time
  • We provide detailed information about each cookie type
  • We honor Do Not Track signals where legally required

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children through cookies. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.
