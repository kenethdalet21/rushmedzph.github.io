Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At RushMedz, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile applications and services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Place orders for medicines or services
  • Upload prescriptions
  • Book medical consultations or appointments
  • Contact customer support
  • Participate in surveys or promotions

This information may include:

  • Full name, email address, and phone number
  • Delivery address and location coordinates
  • Payment information (processed securely through our payment partners)
  • Date of birth and gender
  • Medical information (prescriptions, consultation records)
  • Government-issued IDs (for identity verification)

1.2 Health Information

We collect and process health-related information including:

  • Prescription images and details
  • Medical consultation records
  • Medication history
  • Laboratory test results
  • Allergies and medical conditions

Important: Your health information is treated with the highest level of confidentiality and is only accessible to authorized healthcare professionals and staff who need it to provide services.

1.3 Location Information

For delivery services, we collect real-time location data from:

  • Users (to enable delivery to your location)
  • Drivers (to track deliveries and optimize routes)

1.4 Device and Usage Information

We automatically collect information about how you interact with our apps:

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and browser type
  • App usage patterns and features accessed
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Push notification tokens

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Delivery

  • Processing and fulfilling your orders
  • Coordinating medicine delivery
  • Facilitating telemedicine consultations
  • Booking clinic appointments and lab tests
  • Verifying prescriptions with licensed pharmacists

2.2 Communication

  • Sending order confirmations and delivery updates
  • Providing customer support
  • Sending service announcements and updates
  • Requesting feedback and reviews

2.3 Safety and Security

  • Verifying user identity
  • Preventing fraud and unauthorized access
  • Ensuring prescription authenticity
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity

2.4 Service Improvement

  • Analyzing usage patterns to improve features
  • Personalizing your experience
  • Conducting research and development
  • Optimizing delivery routes and times

2.5 Legal Compliance

  • Complying with Philippine laws and regulations
  • Maintaining records for regulatory requirements
  • Responding to legal requests and investigations

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We share information with trusted third parties who help us operate our services:

  • Partner Pharmacies and Clinics: To fulfill orders and appointments
  • Licensed Doctors: For telemedicine consultations
  • Delivery Drivers: Name, phone, and delivery address for order fulfillment
  • Payment Processors: GCash, PayMaya, PayPal, Stripe (they have their own privacy policies)
  • Cloud Service Providers: For secure data storage and processing
  • SMS/Email Providers: For sending notifications

3.2 Healthcare Providers

Your health information is shared only with:

  • Licensed pharmacists verifying prescriptions
  • Doctors conducting your consultations
  • Healthcare facilities you book appointments with
  • Laboratory partners for test processing

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal process or government requests
  • Protect our rights and property
  • Prevent fraud or security threats
  • Protect public safety

3.4 Business Transfers

If RushMedz is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy protections.

4. Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access to sensitive data
  • Authentication: Multi-factor authentication for staff accounts
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection
  • Regular Audits: Third-party security audits and penetration testing
  • Data Minimization: We only collect what's necessary
  • Secure Infrastructure: Data stored in ISO 27001 certified facilities

Note: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

Under the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012, you have the following rights:

5.1 Right to Access

You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you. Access your data anytime through the app's "Account Settings" or contact us.

5.2 Right to Correction

You can update or correct your personal information directly in the app or request corrections by contacting support.

5.3 Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your account and personal data. Note that we may retain certain information as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., transaction records for tax compliance).

5.4 Right to Data Portability

You can request a machine-readable copy of your personal data to transfer to another service.

5.5 Right to Object

You can object to certain processing activities, such as marketing communications. Use the "unsubscribe" link in emails or adjust preferences in the app.

5.6 Right to Withdraw Consent

Where processing is based on consent, you can withdraw it at any time. This won't affect the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

5.7 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@rushmedz.com
  • Phone: +63 (2) 8888-RUSH
  • In-app: Settings → Privacy → Request Data/Delete Account

We will respond to your request within 30 days as required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

RushMedz is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to improve your experience. See our Cookie Policy for detailed information about:

  • What cookies we use
  • How they work
  • How to manage cookie preferences

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide services and comply with legal obligations:

  • Account Information: While your account is active + 5 years after deletion
  • Transaction Records: 7 years (tax and accounting requirements)
  • Medical Records: 10 years (healthcare regulations)
  • Marketing Data: Until you unsubscribe or object
  • Location Data: 30 days after delivery completion

9. International Data Transfers

Your data is primarily stored and processed in the Philippines. If we transfer data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including:

  • Standard contractual clauses approved by data protection authorities
  • Privacy Shield certification (where applicable)
  • Adequate data protection measures

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of material changes through:

  • In-app notifications
  • Email notifications
  • Prominent notice on our website

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy indicates when it was last revised. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Regulatory Compliance

RushMedz complies with:

  • Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173)
  • National Privacy Commission Circulars and Advisories
  • Department of Health Regulations
  • Food and Drug Administration Requirements
