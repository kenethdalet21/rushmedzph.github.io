Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

Is there any registration fee?

No, registration is completely free. We only charge a small commission on completed orders/bookings.

How much commission does RushMedz charge?

Our commission rates vary by service type: 15-20% for pharmacy orders, 10% for clinic bookings, and 20% for telemedicine consultations. All rates are transparent and clearly stated before you join.

How often will I receive payments?

Payments are processed weekly, every Monday, for all completed orders/bookings from the previous week. Funds are transferred directly to your registered bank account.

Do I need my own delivery fleet?

No, you can use our delivery network. However, if you have your own delivery team, you can integrate them with our system.

Can I set my own prices?

Yes, as a pharmacy or clinic partner, you have full control over your pricing. We recommend competitive pricing to attract more customers.
